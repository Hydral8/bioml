The Core Learning Rule: Local MismatchThe fundamental mechanism for credit assignment in the brain is the calculation of a local error vector at the individual neuron level. Unlike global backpropagation, this occurs through the interaction of segregated information streams.Feedforward Drive ($A_i$): Sensory or bottom-up input typically integrated at the perisomatic/basal zone.Instructive Target ($T_i$): Contextual or top-down feedback integrated at the apical tuft.The Error Signal: Defined as the SD Residual (Somato-Dendritic mismatch).2.
